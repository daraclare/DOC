Seek & Find: Senior Cycle Textbook

Seek & Find is the new Senior Cycle textbook for non-exam religious education, for use by 5th and 6th year students. Working closely with both the authors and editors, this book was designed to appeal to both students and teachers. The text is illustrated thoughout using contemporary photography and artwork.
